Premium Features & Capabilities

📊 Interactive Dashboard

View overall totals for principal and interest at a glance. Manage active and paid loans with quick search filtering, overdue tracking, and payment entry dialogs.

📜 PDF Contract Suite

Generate professional, printable contract PDFs for active or manually entered loans. Customize lender company details, addresses, interest rates, signature dates, and sign directly on-screen with digital ink.

🔄 Quick Re-borrowing

Create new active loans for past borrowers with a single tap. Static information (Name, NRC, Phone, and Workplace) is automatically pre-filled, saving you repetitive data entry.

🔒 PIN & Biometric Security

Secure your financial records with a local PIN lock screen and fingerprint/face authentication, ensuring only authorized access to your private database.

⏰ Persistent Reminders

Schedule daily notifications for overdue payments or reminders one day before due dates. All configured alarms auto-reschedule automatically if the device reboots.

📂 Backups & CSV Exports

Take control of your data. Choose custom export locations on your device, create secure offline JSON backups, and export spreadsheet-compatible CSV reports.
